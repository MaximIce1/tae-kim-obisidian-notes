| **n** |          **w**           | **r** | **y** | **m** | **h** | **n** |     **t**     |    **s**     | **k** |  ー  |    ー    |
| :---: | :----------------------: | :---: | :---: | :---: | :---: | :---: | :-----------: | :----------: | :---: | :-: | :-----: |
|   ん   |            わ             |   ら   |   や   |   ま   |   は   |   な   |       た       |      さ       |   か   |  あ  |  **a**  |
|       |   ゐ^[Not used anymore]   |   ろ   |       |   み   |   ひ   |   に   | ち<br>`(chi)`  | し<br>`(shi)` |   き   |  い  |  **i**  |
|       |                          |   る   |   ゆ   |   む   |   ふ   |   ぬ   | つ <br>`(tsu)` |      す       |   く   |  う  |  **u**  |
|       | ゑ^[Not used anymore]<br> |   れ   |       |   め   |   へ   |   ね   |       て       |      せ       |   け   |  え  | ***e*** |

Mostly ひらがな is used for words which don't have kanji alternative (or if writer decided to not use kanji)

### Small 「ゃ」、「ゅ」、「ょ」

Combining consonant sounds with `a / ya / yu / yo /` is possible with those combinations of kana:

|  p  |  b  |  j  |  g  |  r  |  m  |  h  |  n  |      ch       |       s       |  k  |   ー    |
| :-: | :-: | :-: | :-: | :-: | :-: | :-: | :-: | :-----------: | :-----------: | :-: | :----: |
| ぴゃ  | びゃ  | じゃ  | ぎゃ  | ちゃ  | みゃ  | ひゃ  | にゃ  | ちゃ<br>`(cha)` | しゃ<br>`(sha)` | きゃ  | **ya** |
| ぴゅ  | びゅ  | じゅ  | ぎゅ  | ちゅ  | みゅ  | ひゅ  | にゅ  | ちゅ<br>`(chu)` | しゅ<br>`(shu)` | きゅ  | **yu** |
| ぴょ  | びょ  | じょ  | ぎょ  | ちょ  | みょ  | ひょ  | にょ  | ちょ<br>`(cho)` | しょ<br>`(sho)` | きょ  | **yo** |

### Small 「っ」

Inserting 「っ」between 2 chars to carry consonant sound of the 2nd character to the end of the first one, so it will be doubled / longer / have a pause between them

Example:
	ざっし　（zasshi) ー magazine
	はっぱ　(happa) ー leaf

### Long vowel sounds

「あ」、「い」、「う」can be used to make previous vowel sound longer, depending on the vowel to extend.

| Vowel to extend | Extended by |
| :-------------: | :---------: |
|      / a /      |      あ      |
|    / i / e /    |      い      |
|    / u / o /    |      う      |
Example:
	き　ー＞　きい
	こ　ー＞　こう
	か　ー＞　かあ
